Fun places to visit

Avenida Paulista

You will find a variety of museums, cultural spaces, places to shop, great restaurants and lots of people.

Parque Ibirapuera

In 2017 it was the visited park in Latin america, in addition to being one of the most photographed places in the world. It has sculptures, historical monuments, gardens and landscapes full of flowers and trees, and a huge 55-meters Christmas tree at the end of the year.

Mercado Municipal

Here you will find a large trade in of fruits, vegetables, meats, seasonings and other food products. It is an important historical and architectural building.
